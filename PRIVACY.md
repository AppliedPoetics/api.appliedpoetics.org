# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** May 14, 2026

**Applied Poetics** ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Applied Poetics API and MCP server (collectively, the "Service") at `https://api.appliedpoetics.org`. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Service, including through the Model Context Protocol (MCP) interface.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Text Input
When you use our Service—whether via the REST API or the MCP interface—we receive the text you submit for processing (e.g., words, sentences, or passages you want analyzed, transformed, or constrained).

### Technical Data
We may collect standard server logs, including:
- HTTP request metadata (timestamp, request path, response status)
- Your IP address
- User-agent string

These logs are used for operational monitoring, debugging, and abuse prevention.

## 2. How We Use Information

- **To provide the Service:** We process your text input solely to return the requested analysis or transformation.
- **To maintain and improve:** Server logs help us monitor uptime, diagnose errors, and prevent misuse.
- **We do not:** use your text for training machine-learning models, sell your data, or share it with third parties for marketing or advertising.

## 3. Data Retention

- **Text inputs:** We do **not** persistently store the text you submit. Inputs are held in memory only for the duration of processing and are discarded immediately afterward.
- **Server logs:** Retained for up to 30 days, then automatically deleted.
- **No user accounts:** We do not maintain user profiles or histories.

## 4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. We may disclose information only if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request (e.g., subpoena, court order).

## 5. Third-Party Services

Our Service does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external data we reference are static, bundled dictionaries and term lists (e.g., English words, color names, weather terms) that ship with the application and never leave our servers.

## 6. Security

- All API and MCP traffic is served over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
- The Service is read-only: our tools analyze or transform the text you provide but do not modify any external systems or data stores on your behalf.
- No authentication is required for public use; do not submit sensitive personal information, passwords, or confidential material.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Your Rights

Because we do not store text inputs or maintain user accounts, there is no retained personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have concerns about data we may hold in server logs, contact us (see below) and we will address your request in accordance with applicable law.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
